Privacy policy

Studio Bandit — Last updated: June 2026

‍Who we are
‍Studio Bandit is a design studio registered in the United Kingdom. This website is a portfolio site operated by Studio Bandit. Our registered address is available on request. Questions? Reach us at hello@studiobandit.co.uk.

‍What information we collect
‍We only collect information you choose to give us directly. When you submit an enquiry via our contact form, we collect your name, your email address, and any other information you include in your message. We do not use cookies, tracking scripts, or analytics tools.

‍How we use your information
‍Solely to respond to your enquiry and communicate about potential or ongoing work. We will never use your information for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

‍How we store your information
‍Contact form submissions are received via email and stored securely within our email system. We retain your data only as long as necessary. If you'd like us to delete it, just ask.

‍Who we share your information with
‍Nobody — we do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except where required by law.

‍Your rights under UK GDPR
‍You have the right to access, correct, delete, or object to your data, and to withdraw consent at any time. Email us at hello@studiobandit.co.uk and we'll respond within 30 days. You can also complain to the ICO at ico.org.uk / 0303 123 1113.Changes to this policyWe may update this occasionally. Any changes will be posted here with an updated date.
